How to Become a Broadcast Journalist

An Instruction Challenge for JOUR108

Building Your Skills

Assembly time: 60 minutes
Awesome rating: 99% awesome

What are the ways to succeed as a successful journalist? Here is the practical tips

  • Bottle

  1. Build your interpersonal skills.

    Having strong interpersonal skills and being a well-rounded person are important qualities for a journalist. Take time to get to know your neighbors and community members. Knowing what's important to them can help you get an idea of what news stories might be important for your community. Practice being social with lots of different people from different cultural, economic, religious backgrounds. Become knowledgeable in different cultural or social practices. This can help you connect and understand people,especially in a global context.

  2. Become a good listener.

    Being the type of person that people will want to talk with and confide in will definitely advance your journalism career. If you distinguish yourself as a good listener, people will want to share with you and this can lead to great news scoops or compelling human interest pieces.

  3. Build your rhetoric skills.

    Good broadcast journalists have both strong writing skills in addition to speaking skills. You can improve your writing skills by starting your own blog. Practice writing news or human interest pieces that others will enjoy reading. You might even start to develop a fan base through your blog.

  4. Improve your public speaking skills.

    Public speaking can be scary, but being a good journalist means being able to verbally express yourself and ideas. One way to improve public speaking skills is to practice retelling interesting news stories or current events to your friends and family. Not only can this make for interesting coffee talk, but can help you improve your communication skills. You can also take classes in communications or public speaking.

  5. Learn shorthand.

    Many journalists, especially those working in Europe, claim that knowing shorthand is a useful skill that can help land you a job.
